About Doha Call Line (DCL)

Engineering solutions that build better futures.

Doha Call Line is a Qatar-based multidisciplinary company providing HVAC Solutions, Civil Works, MEP Services and Interior Decoration for residential, commercial and industrial projects.

Discover Our Services

Clear scopes, coordinated execution and reliable project delivery across HVAC, Civil, MEP and Interior Decoration.

HVAC Solutions

Design, supply, installation, testing and maintenance of air conditioning, ventilation and refrigeration systems.

  • Load calculating & system design
  • VRF/VRV, chilled water & DX systems
  • AHU, FCU, FAHU & RAHU installation
  • Ducting, insulation & air distribution
  • Testing, commissioning & maintenance

Civil Works

Structural works, concrete works, masonry, plastering, tiling, and all related civil construction services.

  • Structural works (RCC, steel & masonry)
  • Concrete works & formwork
  • Roads, paving & infrastructure works
  • Excavation, backfilling & compaction
  • Finishing & refurbishment works

MEP Services

Mechanical, electrical & plumbing services including HVAC, electrical systems, plumbing, firefighting and more.

  • Electrical power distribution (LV/MV)
  • Lighting, emergency & exterior lighting
  • Plumbing, sanitary systems
  • Fire alarm, fire fighting & Gas systems
  • BMS, ELV, Data & Communication
  • Testing, commissioning & maintenance

Interior Decoration

Interior design, fit-out, ceiling, flooring, painting, partitions, lighting and all interior decoration works.

  • Interior design & space planning
  • Gypsum works, partitions & ceilings
  • Flooring (tiles, marble, vinyl, carpet, etc)
  • Painting, wallpaper & wall finishes
  • Joinery, carpentry & custom furniture
  • Lighting design & decorative works
